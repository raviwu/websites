# Happy Grandmother's Day

Dear Grandma,  
  
This is the first Mother's Day you've been through after you got your stroke.  
  
I do wish you could be happy and feel good for continuing the living.  
  
It is not easy to face the illness and the feeling of hopeless, but you're brave enough to overcome, i wish you could cross over this mass, i do.  
  
Life is never easy, but it keeps going anyway. I'll try to be your side, please let me know if you need me. We all need someone to stand by our side.  
  
Happy Grandmother's Day.  
  
Grandson, Ravi

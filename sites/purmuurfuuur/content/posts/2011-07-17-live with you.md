You give to me hope  
And help me to cope  
  
When life pulls me down  
You bring me around  
  
  
You teach me to care  
And help me to bear  
  
You make me honest  
With kindness the best  
  
  
It's for you I live  
Thus I want to give  
  
The moment we share  
Delightful joy comes here

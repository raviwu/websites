# family-to-be

![](/images/family.jpg)

It is an ordinary story.

We met and fell in love,

then grow old and cuddle together.

Sadness might come toward the path in the future,

eventually our happiness would cure all struggle.

I am thankful that god brought me faith,

thus you're here, my lord, my love.

# reminder

![](/images/small_51581403.jpg)  
Be open-minded, to see more world.  
  
Be humble, to hear other voices.  
  
Be confident, to speak out thoughts.  
  
  
  
Be not rush, to think deeper.  
  
Be consistant, to make things done.  
  
Be strong, to overcome the failures.  
  
  
  
Be loved, and love back.  
  
Be relax, and enjoy the life.

i am an ordinary person who has a simple dream,  
  
i treat people with honest and basically have belief,  
  
i work hard and be responsible whatever the obstacle i face.  
  
  
unfortunately life is never easy.  
  
  
you can only be stronger when you get hurt,  
  
you shall believe life is wonderful when you're in a mess,  
  
you step back and stand up again when you're knocked down.  
  
  
fortunately you have so-called friend and family.  
  
  
life is short, i still have faith.  
  
recovering from the pain, grow up again.  
  
believing in trust and honor, do things right.
